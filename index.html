About Me

For as long as I can remember, I've loved building things and seeing them work; software engineering lets me pursue that passion with little resources and easy-to-implement iterations. As I've grown up, I've taken a keen interest in financial markets and the movement of money in tandem with predictive models and machine learning as they've steadily taken center stage in the tech world: that's why I pursue Computer Science, Data Science, and Economics, collectively garnering experience in full-stack development, data analysis, and finance applications. I'm quick to learn, a strong collaborator, and motivated to apply problem-solving with modern technology to create valuable products.

When I build, I like to focus on real problems I've seen or know people deal with. I build with the intent to solve problems and streamline complex workflows, with a particular emphasis on the people involved in my work who'll end up using my product, stemming from my own.
